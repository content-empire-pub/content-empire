## Principles

1. **Free content remains free** — articles and previews never go behind a paywall
2. **Quality over quantity** — every paid product must exceed expectations
3. **Transparent pricing** — no hidden fees, clear value proposition
4. **Community first** — supporters get genuine value, not just a badge
5. **Ethical monetization** — no dark patterns, no misleading upsells
